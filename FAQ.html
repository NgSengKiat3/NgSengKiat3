    Frequently Asked Questions (Staff)

    General Information

    Q: Who can access this website?

    A: This website is designed exclusively for staff members of [Company/Organization Name]. Access requires a valid staff login.

    Tasks and Responsibilities

    Q: How do I view my assigned tasks?

    A: After logging in, navigate to the "Tasks" page. Here, you can see your pending, ongoing, and completed tasks.

    Room and Maintenance Management

    Q: How do I report a maintenance issue?

    A: Go to the "Maintenance" page and fill out the maintenance request form. Provide details about the issue and its location.

    Inventory and Supplies

    Q: How do I request additional supplies?

    A: Submit a request through the "Inventory" page. Supervisors will review and approve requests as needed.

    Feedback and Communication

    Q: How do I provide feedback about a task or process?

    A: Navigate to the "Feedback" page and select the relevant category (e.g., Staff Feedback, Guest Feedback). Fill out the form with your suggestions or concerns.
